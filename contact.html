Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Simply navigate to our Services page, choose the service you need, and click "Book Now". You can also call us directly for assistance with booking.

Are your service providers verified?

Yes! All our service providers undergo thorough background checks, reference verification, and skill assessments to ensure the highest quality of service.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking up to 24 hours in advance without any charges. For cancellations within 24 hours, a small fee may apply depending on the service provider's policy.

Do you offer emergency services?

While we don't offer emergency veterinary services, we can help connect you with emergency pet care providers. For immediate medical emergencies, please contact your nearest veterinary clinic.
